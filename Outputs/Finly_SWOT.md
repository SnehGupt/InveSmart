# SWOT Analysis: Finly LBO

**Strengths**
- Strong EBITDA margins and recurring revenue model  
- Scalable SaaS platform with enterprise adoption  
- Low capital expenditure requirements

**Weaknesses**
- High customer acquisition costs  
- Limited geographic diversification  
- Reliance on a few key clients

**Opportunities**
- Expansion into mid-market and international segments  
- Upselling premium features and integrations  
- Strategic partnerships with enterprise platforms

**Threats**
- Intense competition from larger SaaS providers  
- Pricing pressure in saturated markets  
- Potential churn due to economic downturn
